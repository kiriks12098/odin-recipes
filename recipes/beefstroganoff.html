Beef Stroganoff

Beef stroganoff is Russian dish comprised of sauteed beef served in a sauce of mustard and sour cream. Mushrooms are common in many variants. It is often served with egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • Beef strips
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Sour cream
  • Optional: chopped parsley
