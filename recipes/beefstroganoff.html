Beef Stroganoff

Beef stroganoff is Russian dish comprised of sauteed beef served in a sauce of mustard and sour cream. Mushrooms are common in many variants. It is often served with egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • Beef strips
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Sour cream
  • Optional: chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Season the beef and sautee in oil until no longer raw.
  2. Cook the onions, mushrooms and garlic un the same skillet until browned.
  3. Add the beef back into the skillet along with the sour cream. Stir until incorporated
  4. Serve over a bed of egg noodles.
